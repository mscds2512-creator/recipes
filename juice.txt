Simple Blender Juice
Ingredients (for 1 cup)
Fruit: 1 cup of chopped fruit (like watermelon, orange, or pineapple)
Water: 2 tablespoons (only for hard fruits like apples)
Sugar or Honey: 1 teaspoon (optional)
Steps
Chop: Peel the fruit and chop it into pieces. Remove any big seeds.
Blend: Put the fruit, water, and sugar into a blender. Blend until smooth.
Strain: Pour the mixture through a strainer into a glass to remove the pulp.
