Ingredients (for 1 cup)
Water: 1 cup
Tea leaves: 1 teaspoon (or 1 tea bag)
Sugar: 1 teaspoon (optional)
Milk: 2 tablespoons (optional)
Steps
Boil: Heat the water in a small pot until it bubbles.
Steep: Turn off the heat. 
Add the tea leaves, cover the pot, and let it sit for 3 minutes.
Finish: Strain the tea into a cup. Stir in sugar and milk if you like
