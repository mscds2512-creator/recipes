Coffee Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon coffee powder
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 cup water

Steps:
1. Boil the water.
2. Add coffee powder and stir well.
3. Add sugar and mix.
4. Pour in the milk and bring it to a boil.
5. Pour the coffee into a cup.
6. Serve hot.
